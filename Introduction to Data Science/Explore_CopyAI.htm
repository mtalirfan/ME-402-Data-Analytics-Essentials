Lab - Explore CopyAI

Objectives

This lab provides opportunities for learners to explore tools for creative marketing material generation using AI language model provided by the website CopyAI.

Part 1: Explore the CopyAI Website and its Tools

Part 2: Explore Access Options and Pricing Information

Part 3: Sign up and Create a Marketing Product

Background / Scenario

In this lab, you will visit and explore features and products offered by the website https://www.copy.ai/. You will also sign up with a free account to create a marketing product.

Part 3: Sign up and Create a Marketing Product

Step 1: Sign up for a free account.

a.     Sign up a free account using a method of your choosing.

b.     CopyAI will provide you with information for registration. Use the provided information to log into the account.

c.     Provide the necessary information to CopyAI to register a new account.

Step 2: Create a marketing product.

After you have signed up, you can create a new project.

a.     Click Create New Project.

b.     Provide a Project Title or skip this step.

c.     If desired, choose a different tool to create your first product. For example, in the left panel under the Tools heading, click Writing Tools > Freestyle.

d.     In the field Describe your product, provide a short description of your product.

e.     Choose a tone if desired. Click Create Copy for AI generated description.

Question:

Provide the text for your first product. Click Show Answer to view a sample answer.
